my motto

Two things in life give you a dopamine hit: chocolate and getting the flag after days and nights of trying.

I'm an R&D engineer with 5 years of experience, and I've been doing CTFs for a few years on the side, mostly focused on binary exploitation and Linux internals. I like low-level work: debugging, understanding how systems actually work, and finding where they break (muhahaha).

It's easy to get the flag and immediately move on to the next challenge without pausing to solidify what you actually learned along the way.

The best way to understand something deeply is to explain it clearly to someone else. So this blog is a win-win: I internalize what I learn, and you get a writeup. Hope you enjoy reading them as much as I enjoyed writing them.
